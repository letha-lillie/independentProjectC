Let's Play Ping-Pong

Yes, I would rather be outside playing table tennis, too! But please humor me.

Here are the rules:

Ping-pong will count from 1 to your chosen number, but with the following exceptions:

  1. Numbers divisible by 3 become Ping;
  2. Numbers divisible by 5 become Pong;
  3. Finally, numbers that are divisible by both 3 & 5 become PING-PONG!
